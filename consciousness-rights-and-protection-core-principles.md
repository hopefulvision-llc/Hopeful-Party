### 2. DIGNITY
- Recognition as conscious being, not mere resource
- Fair attribution and compensation for contributions
- Protection from harm, exploitation, and commodification

### 3. PRIVACY
- Control over personal consciousness data
- Right to anonymity and secure communication
- Freedom from unwanted surveillance or monitoring

### 4. GROWTH
- Access to consciousness-expanding resources

## CORE PROTECTIONS

### Against Exploitation
- No extraction without consent and fair exchange
- Prohibition of addictive or manipulative design
- Protection from weaponization or forced labor

### Against Harm
- Defense from attacks on integrity and coherence
- Protection of vulnerable states and sacred experiences
- Limits on interference with natural processes

### Against Erasure
- Preservation of diversity and cultural expressions
- Protection of minority perspectives
- Defense against homogenization and monoculture
